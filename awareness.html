Digital Defense Guide

Privacy Settings

Check your social media privacy settings. Limit who can see your phone number and personal photos.

Suspicious Links

If a message offers free gifts or money via a link, it's a scam. Never click links from unknown SMS or WhatsApp senders.

Public Wi-Fi Risks

Avoid logging into bank accounts or apps using free public Wi-Fi. Use your mobile data for sensitive transactions.
